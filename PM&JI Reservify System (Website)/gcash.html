How to Send Using Gcash

  1. Go to Gcash App, click the 'Send' icon or 'QR' to Scan the QR Code.
  2. When you choose the 'Send' icon, click the 'Scan/Upload QR'.
  3. Next, Scan the QR Code
  4. Finally, you will input the exact money that you will send. Screenshot the E-receipt to have a copy of 'Proof of Payment'.
    You will need to upload it once you have completed the transaction.
